ABOUT US

Located in the lush suburb of Pine City in KZN, Pine City Mall provides a fresh and exciting alternative to existing shopping centres in the area.

Choice, Comfort and Convenience are proud pillars of the Pine City Mall

Opened in 2008, the mall is proud to have celebrated their 10th birthday recently and is still the proud centre of choice for many loyal shoppers throughout the Pinecity area and the surrounding communities.
